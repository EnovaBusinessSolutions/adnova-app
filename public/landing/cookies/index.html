Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Adray, Inc. ("Adray," "we," "us," and "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit https://www.adray.ai.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used by website owners to make websites work, work more efficiently, and to provide reporting information.

Cookies set by the site owner are called "first-party cookies." Cookies set by others are called "third-party cookies." Third-party cookies enable third-party features like analytics and advertising.

Why We Use Cookies

We use first-party and third-party cookies for technical, security, analytics, personalization, and advertising purposes. Some cookies are required for the website to function and are considered strictly necessary.

How You Can Control Cookies

You can decide whether to accept or reject cookies through your browser controls and any cookie consent tools available on our website. Strictly necessary cookies cannot be rejected because they are required for core site operation.

Browser-specific help pages: Chrome, Internet Explorer, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Opera.

Cookie Categories We Use

  • Performance and functionality: improve site behavior and user experience.
  • Analytics and personalization: help us understand traffic, usage patterns, and campaign effectiveness.
  • Advertising: make ads more relevant and measure ad performance.
  • Unclassified: cookies that are pending classification.

Examples of Cookies on Adray.ai

The exact cookies may vary over time and by region, but examples include:

  • Performance: MR (Microsoft / Bing / Clarity)
  • Analytics: _ga, _ga_* (Google Analytics), _clck, _clsk, _cltk, CLID, SM, MUID, c.gif
  • Advertising: SRM_B, ANONCHK, test_cookie, _gcl_au, _gcl_ls, _fbp, lastExternalReferrer, lastExternalReferrerTime
  • Unclassified: ri-*

Other Tracking Technologies

We may also use web beacons (including tracking pixels and clear GIFs) to understand traffic flows, campaign effectiveness, and interactions with pages and emails.

Flash Cookies / Local Shared Objects

Some technologies may use local shared objects. Limiting these in your browser/player settings may affect certain website features.

Targeted Advertising

Third parties may place cookies to show relevant ads and measure ad performance across websites. This information typically does not directly identify you unless you provide identifying information.

Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this policy from time to time for legal, operational, or technical reasons. The "Last updated" date reflects the latest revision.
