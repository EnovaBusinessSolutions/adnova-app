ABOUT

The Data Layer Behind Ecommerce

We reconcile Meta, Google, and Shopify into one source of truth — so merchants finally know what's actually driving revenue.

Every ad platform tells a different story. Merchants deserve a single source of truth. We built Adray to be the invisible data layer that reconciles it all — then hands it to the AI tools merchants already use.
OUR VALUES

We are committed

The principles that guide how we build Adray.

Own the Data, Not the Dashboard

We believe the data layer is worth more than any UI built on top of it. We own collection and reconciliation — merchants choose the tools.

Free at Scale Beats Paid with Better Features

Our free tier scales to 100k merchants at near-zero cost. Every connected pixel strengthens the network for everyone.

Invisible Infrastructure, Real Results

The best outcome is a merchant who never thinks about us — they just know their AI gives accurate answers and their agents run reliably.

OUR FOUNDER

From the Founder

Why we pivoted from dashboard to data infrastructure.

“We started building a dashboard — then realized the real value was underneath it. Every ad platform tells merchants a different story. Meta says 40x ROAS. Google says 8x. Shopify shows 12 orders. Nobody knows what's real. We stopped competing with AI interfaces and became the data layer they depend on. Today, Adrayreconciles the truth across platforms and hands it to the AI tools merchants already use. The best infrastructure is invisible — and that's exactly what we're building.”
